Help & Documentation

Home

Dashboard showing total net worth, investments, cash, loans, and insurance. Click section cards to navigate to different categories. View your financial summary at a glance.

Summary

Financial Dashboard: View key metrics including Net Worth, Portfolio ROI, Savings Rate, and Monthly Expenses with visual cards.

Interactive Charts: Net Worth Trend Chart shows your wealth growth over time with visual insights.

Sunburst Charts: Visual representation of investments, loans, and insurance distribution across categories.

Reports: Download Excel reports for all sections including portfolio, FDs, bonds, commodities, crypto, insurance, real estate, and loans.

Stocks & Mutual Funds

Track equity and mutual fund investments. Add holdings with name, quantity, purchase price, and current price. Filter by category (Stocks, Equity MF, Debt MF, Hybrid MF, US Equity, US Debt). Live prices update automatically if API is configured. View gains/losses, investment value, and current value for each holding.

Cash & Savings

Manage all bank accounts. Add account with type (Savings/Current), bank name, account number, and balance. Account balances update automatically when you add expense transactions. View total cash across all accounts and individual account details.

Expense Tracker

Record all financial transactions (credit/debit). Select account (cash/bank/credit card), category, amount, date, and description. Filter by date range (last 7/30/90 days or custom). Transactions automatically update bank and credit card balances. Categories include Food, Transportation, Shopping, Bills, Healthcare, Entertainment, and more.

Credit Cards

Manage credit cards with card name, limit, and due date. Credit limit usage is automatically calculated from expense tracker. Pay bills (full/partial) from any bank account. Bill payments create debit entries in expense tracker and credit entries in credit card transactions.

Fixed Deposits

Track FDs with bank name, principal amount, interest rate (annual %), tenure (months), start date. Maturity date is calculated automatically. View maturity amounts, interest earned, and days remaining. Interest is compounded quarterly as per standard FD calculations.

Bonds

Track government and corporate bonds. Add bond name/issuer, face value, coupon rate (annual %), and maturity date. View annual interest calculations, maturity amounts, and days remaining until maturity. Monitor your fixed income portfolio.

Commodities

Track physical assets like gold, silver, platinum, and other commodities. Add commodity name, quantity, unit (grams/kg/ounces), purchase price per unit, and current price. View total investment value, current value, and absolute gains/losses with percentage returns.

Cryptocurrency

Track digital asset investments. Add cryptocurrency name, ticker symbol (BTC, ETH, etc.), quantity/coins, and average purchase price. View current value, investment value, and gains/losses. Monitor your entire crypto portfolio in one place.

Insurance

Track life and health insurance policies. Add policy type, provider/company, policy number, premium amount, payment frequency (Monthly/Quarterly/Annual), coverage start/end dates, and sum assured. View total coverage amount and annual premium costs.

Other Investments

Track government schemes and other investments like PPF, EPF, NPS, NSC, Sukanya Samriddhi, SCSS, Atal Pension Yojana, Sovereign Gold Bonds, and more. Select from 15+ predefined schemes or add custom investments. Track investment types, payout types (Monthly/Quarterly/Annual/Maturity), investment dates, amounts, interest rates, and tenures. Maturity dates and returns are automatically calculated for schemes with fixed tenure.

Real Estate

Track property investments and assets. Add property name, type (Residential/Commercial/Land), location/address, purchase price, current market value, purchase date, and rental income (if applicable). View appreciation in value, returns percentage, and total rental income received.

Loans

Track all loans including home, car, personal, education, and business loans. Add loan type, lender name, loan amount, interest rate (annual %), EMI amount, start date, tenure (months), and outstanding amount. View payment schedules, total interest payable, amount paid so far, and months remaining.

Calculators

Available Calculators:

  • SIP Calculator: Calculate returns on Systematic Investment Plans with monthly contributions
  • Lumpsum Calculator: Calculate returns on one-time investments
  • FD Calculator: Calculate fixed deposit maturity amounts with quarterly compounding
  • EMI Calculator: Calculate loan EMI, total interest, and payment schedule
  • STP Calculator: Calculate Systematic Transfer Plan from debt to equity funds
  • SWP Calculator: Calculate Systematic Withdrawal Plan for regular income
  • FIRE Calculator: Calculate Financial Independence Retire Early timeline
  • Tax Calculator: Compare old vs new tax regime with deductions (80C, 80D, 24b)
  • Portfolio Rebalancing: Analyze portfolio deviation and get buy/sell recommendations

All calculators provide detailed breakdowns with charts and projections.

Settings

Features:

  • Dark Mode: Toggle dark/light theme for comfortable viewing
  • Session Timeout: Automatic logout after 15 minutes of inactivity for security
  • Live Price API: Configure Alpha Vantage API for real-time stock prices
  • Change Password: Update your account password
  • Export Data: Download all your data as JSON backup file
  • Import Data: Restore data from backup file

Alerts & Notifications

Receive automatic alerts for important events: FD maturity within 30 days, Bond maturity within 30 days, Budget exceeded warnings, and Goal achievement milestones. Check the notification badge for pending alerts.

Budget & Goals

Budget Management: Set monthly budgets for expense categories. Track spending and receive alerts when exceeding limits. View spending vs budget comparison charts.

Financial Goals: Set savings goals with target amount and deadline. Track progress towards goals like house down payment, emergency fund, vacation, education, etc. View completion percentage and receive milestone notifications.

AI Chatbot Assistant

Get instant help and answers to your financial questions with the AI-powered chatbot. Click the chat icon in the bottom-right corner to start a conversation. Ask about features, get guidance on using the app, or receive personalized financial tips. The chatbot remembers your conversation history within the session.

Contact & Feedback

Need help or have suggestions? Access the Contact section from the main menu to send us an email or provide feedback through our feedback form. We value your input and continuously work to improve the app based on user suggestions.

• Check Summary section for comprehensive financial analysis and reports
• Use the AI chatbot for instant help and guidance

Important Data Storage Notice

Your Data is Stored Locally

All your financial data is stored locally in your browser's storage. This provides complete privacy and security as your data never leaves your device.

⚠️ Warning: If you clear your browser data, cache, or cookies, all your financial information will be permanently deleted!

How to Protect Your Data:

  • Regularly export your data using Settings → Data Management → Export Data
  • Save the backup file in a safe location (Cloud storage, USB drive, etc.)
  • Never clear browser data without backing up first
  • Use the same browser and device for consistency
